What is nutritional counseling?

Assessing the specific needs of every client and integrating that into a functional working diet, using local foods. The goal is to ensure a better and wholesome understanding of nutrition, providing your body with all the required nourishment, and preventing deficiency.

What problems/issues can be treated?

  • Nutritional deficiency
  • Metabolic issues
  • Chronic inflammation and
  • inflammation-related illness
  • Obesity and weight loss
  • What are the techniques offered?
  • Nutritional planning and diet chart
  • Daily online assistance
  • Monthly re-evaluations and goal setting
