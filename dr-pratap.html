Dr. Prathap Addageethala

Striving For Excellence In Spine Care



Being a first-generation Canadian, born to immigrant Indian parents, I was the first of the family to become a doctor. You can only imagine how awesome that felt! My dream was unique - I wanted to bring my talents to India, where Chiropractic was largely unheard of, unless you watched the TV sitcom Two and a Half Men.

Today, as an expert in soft tissue mechanics, part of the Indian Association of Chiropractic Doctors, as well as the Director and Head of Chiropractic for Atlas Chiropractic and Wellness, I’m able to bring the benefits of Chiropractic to many more people. I am proud to say that I kick start the healing process in my patients in a safe and process-driven manner. Being one of the few licensed and certified Chiropractors in India is a privilege and a responsibility that I take very seriously.

While it gives me great joy to wake up in the morning and go help people out, I also realize how important it is to empower people and show them how to help themselves recover from whatever issue they are facing. It’s the whole “give a man a fish/teach a man to fish” idea - I believe that the patient must take an active role in that recovery process. I push my patients to commit to their health, to go beyond where they are currently. It’s not magic, it’s Chiropractic, and it works!
